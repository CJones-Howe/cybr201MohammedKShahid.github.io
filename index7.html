Here are some fun facts about pollution in the ocean

  • Scientists have estimated that there are around 171 trillion pieces of plastic pollution in the ocean.
  • Plastic takes around 500 to 1000 years to degrade. After that, it still becomes microplastics, without actually fully degrading.
  • It is estimated that by the year 2050 there will be more plastic in the ocean than there our fish on the planet.
  • Plastic microbeads, that are in face washes are small enough to be eaten by marine wildlife.
  • The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is a collection of trash debris in the pacific ocean, and it is estimated to be twice the size of Texas.
